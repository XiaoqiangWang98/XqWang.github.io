Biography

I am currently a PhD student in School of Automation of Xi'an Jiaotong University(XJTU), supervised by Prof.Liangjun Ke. I obtained my B.E. degree in Automation from Chongqing University(CQU), Chongqing, P.R. China, in 2018.

My recent research interests are on Reinforcement Learning, Multi-agent Leanrning.
